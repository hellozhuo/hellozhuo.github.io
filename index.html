Zhuo Su

I am a final-year PhD candidate at Center for Machine Vision and Signal Analysis (CMVS) in University of Oulu, Finland. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning. My doctoral thesis topic is efficient feature representation learning and its applications in computer vision.

During my PhD training, I have visited AMLab, University of Amsterdam, working as a visiting researcher supervised by Max Welling. Currently, I am a computer vision intern in the Embodied AI Lab at Intel, Germany, working with Matthias Müller. Before that, I received my master's degree and bachelor's degree from Beihang University in 2015 and 2018 respectively.
